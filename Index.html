Areas

In Parc D'Daluxe there are a lot of cool areas to explore. There are 4 areas in total. Adventure Park, Thrill Zone, Dophin Pool and Family Playground.

Adventure Park

Adventure park is the biggest area of Parc D'Daluxe. It features a lot of attractions. For both thrill seekers and families. Walk around and explore all the different themes, rides and shows made for every age.

Thrill Zone

Thrill Zone features some of thr scariest snd biggest rides in the whole world. Take the legendary Kingda King for example. 300 km/h in just 5 seconds. Or the Doom Tower which will fall you over 600ft. If you're a thrilseeker then this place is for you!

Dolphin Pool

Swim, slide and splash in the Dolphin Pool. The biggest water park in all of Florida! Explore more than 35 slides, 7 pools and countless activities to make your day at Dolphin Pool complete.

Family Playground

A paradise for small children. That's the best definiton of Family Playground on Parc D'Daluxe. With numerous playgrounds and countless activities, its the best place for your little child to play while you explore the rest of the park.
